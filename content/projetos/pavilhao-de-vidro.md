description_en: |
  The Glass Pavilion is an exercise in transparency and structural lightness. Located in the heart of a private garden in the Algarve, this pavilion functions as a refuge for meditation and reading.

  The minimal steel structure supports large panels of thermal glass that eliminate visual barriers between interior and exterior. At night, the pavilion becomes a lantern of soft light, integrated into the surrounding vegetation.
