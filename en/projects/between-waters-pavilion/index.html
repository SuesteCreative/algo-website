The Concept

The Between Waters Pavilion is strategically positioned between two mirror-like water surfaces. The wooden and exposed concrete structure creates a play of reflections and shadows that changes throughout the day.

The goal was to create a fluid transition between leisure areas and the surrounding landscape, using tactile materials and a minimalist language that prioritizes visual silence and thermal comfort.
