Civic Center

Location Berlin, Germany
Year 2022
Area 2500 m²
Status Completed

The Concept

The Berlin Civic Center is a space dedicated to community and cultural life. The project is based on the creation of a large covered square with free access, which functions as an extension of urban public space.

The sobriety of the materials — gray brick, concrete, and wood — reflects the rigor and permanence of the institution. The design focuses on the flexibility of interior spaces, allowing for exhibitions, conferences, and various social events.
