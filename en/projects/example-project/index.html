Single Family Housing

Penedo House

Location

Braga, Portugal

Year

2024

Area

320 sqm

Concept and Approach

Penedo House emerges as a direct response to the terrain's steep topography. The project is based on the idea of a monolithic concrete volume that suspends over the slope, allowing the landscape to flow beneath the construction.

The spatial organization privileges natural light and the visual relationship with the horizon. The use of raw materials — concrete, wood, and glass — reinforces construction honesty and integration into the rocky context.
