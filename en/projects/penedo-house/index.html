Penedo House

Location Braga, Portugal
Year 2024
Area 320 m²
Status Completed

The Concept

Penedo House emerges as a direct response to the rugged topography of the terrain. The project is based on the idea of a monolithic concrete volume that suspends over the slope, allowing the landscape to flow under the construction.

The spatial organization prioritizes natural light and the visual relationship with the horizon. The use of raw materials — concrete, wood, and glass — reinforces the constructive honesty and integration in the rocky context, creating a symbiosis between the built and the natural.
