The Concept

The Tower of Light is a vertical landmark that challenges Madrid's urban density. The project uses a dynamic skin of metal fins that capture and reflect sunlight differently throughout the day.

The building combines residential, commercial, and cultural spaces into a single volume, promoting a diversity of uses and urban vitality. The central atrium, which spans the entire height of the tower, functions as a lung of natural light and ventilation.
